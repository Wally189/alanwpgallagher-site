Fáilte. I’m Alan, and this is my digital home.

You are most welcome.

Some things here are practical: professional material, my CV, and a simple way to get in touch. There are other layers too — ideas, projects, and the strands of my life that shape how I work.

With experience, I have found that I am drawn to order, method, and work done carefully and well. These instincts are often dressed up in different ways — Kaizen, Lean, Agile — or repackaged in self-help language. I’ve used some of these approaches, and still do. Today, my work is grounded in ways of thinking that are much older, and in my view, more proven.

I’m interested in slow technology: tools and systems that protect time, dignity, and clarity.
